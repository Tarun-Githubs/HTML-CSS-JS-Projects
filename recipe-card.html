Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with grated parmesan
  4. Drain pasta and mix with pancetta and egg mixture
  5. Serve hot with extra cheese
